Encounter underway in J&K's Budgam district


Updated: Mar 28, 2017, 12.39 PM IST

One person was killed and four others were injured when security forces took action against stone-pelting protesters The protesters had gathered near the site of the encounter The encounter is still underway as per latest reports NEW DELHI: One person was killed and four other were wounded after an encounter broke out between terrorists and security forces in Chadoora area in Jammu & Kashmir's Budgam district early on Tuesday morning

Security forces launched a cordon and search operation in Durbagh locality in Chadoora area following information about the presence of terrorists, a police official said. "The search operation turned into a gunbattle after militants opened fire on security forces," the official said, adding that the exchange of firing was going on when last reports came in. The fatality occurred when stone-pelting protesters gathered near the site of the encounter and security forces took action against them. A protestor was hit by a bullet in the neck and succumbed to his injuries on the way to a hospital, the police official said.

Four others suffered injuries as security forces fired pellet guns and tear gas shells in a bid to disperse the protesters, the official said. On Sunday, two Hizbul Mujahideen terrorists were killed when they tried to ambush a police convoy on the border of Awantipura and Pulwama districts in south Kashmir.
